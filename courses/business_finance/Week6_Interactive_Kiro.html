LAB 1

Debt vs Equity Cost Comparison 债务与股权成本比较

Controls

Equity required return (Ke) 14%
Debt coupon rate (Kd) 8%
Tax rate (t) 30%
Debt proportion (d) 40%

Results

After-tax cost of debt = Kd × (1 − t)
5.60%
Cost of equity (Ke)
14.00%
WACC = Ke×(1−d) + Kd×(1−t)×d
10.64%
Tax shield saving = Kd × t × d
0.96%
Debt is cheaper because of the tax shield.
WACC = 10.64%
LAB 2

Convertible Bond Valuation 可转换债券估值

Controls

Bond face value £100
Conversion ratio (shares/bond) 40
Current share price £2.20
Coupon rate 5%

Results

Conversion price = Face value ÷ Conversion ratio
£2.50
Conversion value = Ratio × Share price
£88.00
Conversion premium
13.64%
Floor value = max(straight bond, conversion value)
£100.00
Conversion is not yet worthwhile — share price is below the conversion price. Bondholders prefer to hold for coupon income.
LAB 3

Rights Issue Calculator 配股计算器

Controls

Current share price £3.20
Rights issue price £2.50
Rights ratio N (N-for-1) 4

Results

TERP = (N × Current price + Issue price) ÷ (N+1)
£3.06
Value of right = TERP − Issue price
£0.56
Discount to TERP
4.38%
Dilution effect (price drop)
£0.14
TERP is the theoretical ex-rights price — the fair value after new shares are issued. Existing shareholders are compensated via the value of the right, so wealth is preserved regardless of whether they exercise or sell their rights.
